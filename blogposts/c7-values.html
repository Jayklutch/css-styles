Willpower

Willpower - I believe in a mind before matter universe, that there is no objective truth in this world, it being an illusion projected from a deeper and more true reality that our humble senses cannot begin to fathom, but that our imagination can. Willpower is important in this equation / paragdim as imagination is a flightly thing that jumps from one angle to another suppose and back to a spiral without any assistance. Help comes from willpower, which can direct and guide imagination. This allows our minds to contimplate a deeper and more truer sense of reality than is normally availible to the untrained mind.

Advice

The last thing that someone asked me for advice on was cooking. I gave the advice that I normally give in these situatons which is "If you thing it's the right thing to do, give it a go, BUT, remember what you were unsure of, what you did and what were/are the results from that action. Then next time and the time after, recall what you've did and let those results be your guide".

Can you think of any other exercises you can use to help you when you are feeling down (for whatever reason) at EDA?

I have carried this with me since I read it as a nine year old and it has helped me through many bad patches were I've feared for my life or just needed a boost:

I must not fear.

Fear is the mind-killer.

Fear is the little-death that brings total obliteration.

I will face my fear.

I will permit it to pass over me and through me.

And when it has gone past I will turn the inner eye to see its path.

Where the fear has gone there will be nothing.

Only I will remain
